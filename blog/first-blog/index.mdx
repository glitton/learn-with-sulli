It's Sunday morning on _Bank Street_
Let's go out for a **walk**
Pull on boots or sandals
and **talk**

- You can wear your jacket or not
- We'll drink iced coffee or order it hot

Me, mysterious, in my new shades. You not so much in your old hat.
